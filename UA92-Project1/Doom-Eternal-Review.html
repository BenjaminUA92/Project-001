Doom Eternal: A Heart-Pounding Thrill Ride with Stunning Combat

14/04/23 10 min

Doom Eternal is an adrenaline-fueled ride that will keep you on the edge of your seat from start to finish. The game's fast-paced, intense combat is a true standout, with a wide range of weapons and abilities that allow for an incredible level of customization and strategy. The graphics and sound design are also top-notch, with a level of detail that truly immerses you in the game's hellish world.

One of the most impressive aspects of Doom Eternal is its level design. Each level is carefully crafted to provide a unique and engaging experience, with a perfect balance between exploration and combat. The game's boss fights are also a true highlight, with epic battles that will leave you breathless.

However, there are a few issues that should be noted. Some players may find the game's difficulty to be too punishing, particularly in the later stages of the game. Additionally, the game's story can sometimes feel a bit convoluted, with a lot of lore to unpack and not always clear direction on where to go next. Finally, while the game's multiplayer features are a welcome addition, they can sometimes suffer from connectivity issues.
